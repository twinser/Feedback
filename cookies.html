Cookies and Privacy

What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files stored on your computer by websites.

How does this website use cookies?
This website stores cookies on your computer to keep you logged in.
It also uses them to store your survey answers before submission.
All cookies have a maximum life of one hour or are deleted when you log out. None of your personal data is stored.

Will this website work without cookies?
No. Unfortunately, it is currently necessary to use cookies to keep you logged in.
